Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 3, 2026

At Mailto Link Generator, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use our free online tool.

Our tool is designed to be privacy-first. We do not collect, store, or share any of the information you enter into the generator fields (emails, subject lines, or body text). All processing and link generation happen locally within your web browser using JavaScript.

2. Cookies and Tracking

We may use minimal, anonymous analytical cookies to understand how visitors interact with our site. This helps us improve the user experience. These cookies do not store personally identifiable information.

Our website may contain links to other sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external websites.

4. Security

Since no data is transmitted to our servers for the generation of links, your input remains private on your own device. However, please be aware that any information sent via an email client after clicking a mailto link is subject to your email provider's privacy policy.

5. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this policy at any time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
